Getting Started with Brave Rewards

Follow these steps to maximize your rewards:

  • Enable Brave Rewards:
    1. Go to Settings > Brave Rewards
    2. Turn it on to start earning Basic Attention Token (BAT)
  • Use Brave on Multiple Devices: 📱 Earning BAT is separate for each device with Brave Rewards enabled.
  • Use Brave Search & Engage with Content: 🔍️ Using Brave Search and engaging with privacy-respecting ads can increase rewards.
  • Tip & Get Tipped: 💸 BAT can be used to tip verified Brave creators on YouTube, Twitter, Reddit, and more.
  • Participate in Brave Promotions & Grants: 🎁 Brave occasionally offers BAT grants to new users—check your Rewards page!
