🎮 Gaming Subscription Comparison

Updated: Includes pricing, trial availability, game access rules, and whether games are permanently retained.

🔴 Netflix Games

  • Price: Included with Netflix subscription (starting at €7.99/month)
  • Free Trial: 30-day free trial for new users
  • Game Access: Access to mobile games on iOS and Android; available while subscribed
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No
  • Additional Benefits: Ad-free gaming, no in-app purchases

🟡 Google Play Pass

  • Price: €4.99/month or €29.99/year
  • Free Trial: 1-month free trial for new subscribers
  • Game Access: Premium games/apps on Android, ad-free, no in-app purchases
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No

🔶 Apple Arcade

  • Price: €4.99/month
  • Free Trial: 1-month free trial for new subscribers
  • Game Access: 200+ exclusive games on iOS/macOS/tvOS; playable while subscribed
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No

🔷 Humble Choice

  • Price: $11.99/month
  • Free Trial: Occasional limited-time offers for new users
  • Game Access: Monthly curated PC games to keep permanently
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ✔️ Yes
  • Additional Benefits: Humble Games Collection access + up to 20% store discounts

🟥 Ubisoft+

  • Price: €14.99/month
  • Free Trial: Occasionally offers 7-day trials
  • Game Access: Full Ubisoft library including new/premium titles; playable while subscribed
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No

🟢 Amazon Prime Gaming

  • Price: €4.99/month or €49.99/year (Netherlands)
  • Free Trial: 30-day free trial available
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ✔️ Yes – Claimed games are yours to keep permanently

🟦 Xbox Game Pass Ultimate

  • Price: $16.99/month
  • Free Trial: Occasional 14-day trial offers (e.g. $1 deals)
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No – Access ends with subscription

🔵 PlayStation Plus

  • Price: Varies by tier (Essential, Extra, Premium)
  • Free Trial: No current trial available
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ❌ No – Access ends with subscription

🟣 Epic Games Store

  • Price: Free – no subscription required
  • Free Trial: Not applicable
  • Games Retained After Subscription Ends: ✔️ Yes – Regular Free games can be permanently added to your library
